Welcome to the scale selector app! This thing will tell you all the names of the notes in a given scale.

Here is how it works: On the right side of the screen you will see the scale selector. Click the button that corresponds to the name of the scale you are trying to learn, and the notes of that scale will be displayed in the pitches area on the left.

PITCHES
0 - (tonic)

1 - (flat 2)

2 - (supertonic)

3 - (flat 3)

4 - (mediant)

5 - (subdominant)

6 - (flat 5)

7 - (dominant)

8 - (minor 6)

9 - (major 6)

10 - (flat 7)

11 - (leading tone)

Click on one of these buttons to see all the notes of that scale displayed by the names of their scale degrees.
