Aaron was born in Everett General Hospital in North Everett, attended preschool on 19th st, elementary school at Monroe Elementary and View Ridge Elementary, then Eisenhower Middle School, Peaceful Glen Christian School in Snohomish, Cascade High School, Issaquah High School, Bellevue College where he studied choral singing and music theater.

He started studying piano with his mother at a young age. Students of all ages would come to the house and he would listen to them play the music of the great composer at all levels of proficiency. In 6th grade he started clarinet, tried flute briefly, tried playing guitars,
