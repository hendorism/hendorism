Lawn Installation

Is your lawn looking unhealthy, with bare spots and patches of dead lawn? It may be time for a lawn replacement. NW Landscape Management, Inc. offers sod replacement for new lawn installation. New lawn areas provide a surface that is resilient, easily trafficked and aesthetically pleasing. Installation of new sod is the quickest way to create a new lawn. We use locally sourced sod featuring grass varieties that perform well in our climate. Installed properly, you can have a brand new lawn that is fully functional in 30 days. Our service includes removing old lawn areas, bringing in Greensmix, which is a sandy-soil mixture that provides a good growing base for the sod to establish, and delivery and installation of the new sod. Contact us with your square footage, and we can give you approximate costs for this service.

Paver and Flagstone Patios

Hardscapes are a beautiful addition to any landscape. NW Landscape Management is a licensed, bonded, and insured landscape contractor that specializes in hardscape installation. A paver or flagstone patio is a wonderful accent piece to your landscape, or a center piece to your outdoor living space. Paver and flagstone patios are easy to take care of and can withstand the Pacific Northwest weather. There are many varieties of flagstone and pavers to choose from with varying colors and textures. Our attention to detail in the construction of hardscaping ensures a well-built patio—we carefully excavate the area, fill in and compact the base material, before setting the pavers or flagstone. We can also add fire-pits to your patio areas, adding comfort and ambiance to your outdoor areas.

In addition to patios, pavers can be used for walkways, driveways and driveway extensions. We also use block to install small retaining walls (less than 4 feet) and garden walls for terracing and raised garden beds. In additional we install stone treads and paver stairs to address sloped areas of your property. We can also install outdoor kitchen areas for expanding your entertaining spaces. There are several options and styles to choose from, using cultured stone exteriors to create a unique feature.

Sprinkler System – Irrigation System Installation

At NW Landscape Management, Inc, we design and install sprinkler systems which eliminate watering hassles for your lawn during our dry summers. A sprinkler system has great features including programmable controllers, commercial grade piping and sprinkler heads, and optional rain sensors. Let NW Landscape Management install a completely automatic sprinkler system that allows you to spend time enjoying your beautiful landscape instead of watering it.

What are the benefits of an automatic sprinkler system?

Landscape sprinkler irrigation systems are a great addition to any landscape. There are many benefits to having an automatic sprinkler system in your lawn–the convenience of not manually moving a lawn sprinkler around, no worries about having time to water each day, more efficient use of water and the ability to fully schedule your watering program. Call us now to get these sprinklers installed in your garden.

Sprinkler Winterization Service includes air-compression removal of all water in the lines to prevent damage to pipes from freezing water.

Landscape Lighting

Landscape lighting will transform your landscape into a work of art, as your property will come alive at night with the soft glow of a low voltage landscape lighting system.

The time your landscape is usable will be extended–outdoor kitchens, hot tubs, children’s play areas, and ponds all become more functional.

Pathways, staircases, grade changes, low hanging vegetation and dark areas can be lit up to make your landscape more safe to navigate at night.

Also, having your landscape illuminated at night can add a level of security to your property.
