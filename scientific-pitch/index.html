scientific pitch notation!

In scientific pitch notation every octave has a number. Middle C is in the 4th octave, and the frequency of that note is about 261hz. Every octave begins on C and goes up D E F G A B.

The octave numbers also go in the negative direction.
How many times can you divide a number by 2 before the answer becomes zero?
https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/4/41/Scientific_pitch_notation_octaves_of_C.png

To derive all the pitches in the equal tempered scale I use the equation 440*2^(n/12).

440 is the frequency of A4. Every time you go up 12 half-steps the frequency doubles. n is the number of half-steps above A4.
