aaron's music dojo
Aaron's music dojo is a place where you can go to hone your music skills. There are many challenges there. You will learn how to read music. You will learn to write music. You will learn how to write down the music you hear in your head or on the radio or in your dreams. In doing this you will have artistically brought a very authentic part of yourself out into the world in sharrper focus than before, and you have added value to society. This is the basic value proposition of music. It's all about bein' real.

-Aaron Dull

Innovations Studio

I made this site so I could have a site to post music and blog posts, but I quickly became displeased with the way the site builder works. So I started this github page as soon as I discovered that github will host a live url for free. very soon I will spend the money to get a real website.
